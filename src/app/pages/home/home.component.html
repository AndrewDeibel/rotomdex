Open Beta

Rotomdex is currently in open beta, create a free account today and help improve stability during this stage. Please consider reporting issues through discord.

A free account does have limited scanning capacity and collection features. Additional features can be unlocked by signing up for our premium subscription (Profile > Subscription) that includes additional scanning capacity and unlimited collection size.

Expansions

Explore the most recent expansions, vintage expansions, and everything in-between. Rotomdex provides the latest card and set data for both English and Japanese (coming soon).

Cards

View every Pokemon card printed! Sort by artist, release date, Pokemon, rarity, or card type. Everyone has a favorite card but with 14,000+ unique cards there is always new cards to appreciate.

Pokemon

View the cards featuring your favorite Pokemon and explore the ever-growing Pokedex.

Collection

Manage your entire Pokemon card collection! Organize cards using binders, decks, and other grouping categories. View and share your collection stats and history. Included card metadata:

  • Condition (mint, near mint, graded 10, graded 9.5, etc)
  • Grading company (PSA, BGS, CGC, etc)
  • Unique printings (1st edition, reverse foil, etc)
  • Price (acquired price, market price)
  • Notes (memories, history, misc)
  • Group (binder, deck, box)
  • Wishlist
